A Bit About Me

I was born and raised in Bangladesh and have over four years of professional experience. I am currently a final-year Ph.D. candidate at the University of Central Florida, where my research focuses on developing implementable and scalable data-driven solutions for intelligent transportation systems, using deep learning, causal inference, and statistical techniques.

Outside of work, sports have always been a significant part of my life. I have been actively involved in soccer and cricket, competing in and winning numerous tournaments over the years, both as a player and a team leader.

I thrive in high-pressure competitive environments, whether it's leading my team on the field or collaborating on innovative data science projects.
